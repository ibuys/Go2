Personal Thanks

I would like to thank you again, for reading this document, and for using Go2. I sincerely hope that Go2 makes your life just a tiny bit easier, and maybe makes getting things done just a little bit faster.

I thank my wife, for her support, for her encouragement, and for her understanding of why I needed to stay up to 2 A.M. writing code. Without her constantly being there for me, neither Farmdog Software, nor Go2 would exist.

I’d like to thank Jim Turner who I met through our local CocoaHeads chapter in Des Moines. Without his expert guidance, freely and generously given for months, Go2 would not be what it is today. Although Jim has moved away to California for bigger and better things, he remains a source of encouragement and enlightenment.

I would also like to thank Daniel Jalkut, developer of MarsEdit and founder of Red Sweater Software. He left an encouraging note on a blog post of mine. If I see you at WWDC, I’ll buy you a beer.
