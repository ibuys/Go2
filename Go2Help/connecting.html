Connecting to Bookmarks

To open a bookmarked connection, highlight the bookmark and press “return” on the keyboard.

You can also open a bookmarked connection by choosing “File” and then “Open Bookmark” from the menu.

You can also select multiple bookmarks and launch all of the connections at once.

Go2 will ask OS X to choose the appropriate application to handle the connection. If you would like to change what application handles a specific protocol, set that application as the system default.
