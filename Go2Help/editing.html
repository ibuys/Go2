Editing Your Bookmarks

When you need to change a bookmark in Go2, you can edit the information by highlighting your bookmark, selecting “Edit”, and then “Host Info” from the menu. Optionally, you can also edit the bookmark information by pressing the keyboard combination “⌘ i”.
