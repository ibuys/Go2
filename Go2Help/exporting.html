Exporting Your Bookmarks

If you would like to make a backup of your bookmarks, Go2 supports exporting bookmarks as a plain text file, identical to the one it will import. Go2 will export a plain text file containing one URL per line, ready to be imported whenever you like.

To export a text file, click on “File” and then “Export Bookmarks”.
