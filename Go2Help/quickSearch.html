Quick Search

Go2 includes a status bar item which appears to the left of the Spotlight, time, and wireless icons. If you click on the tiny coffee cup icon Go2 will drop down a search field, with the cursor ready for you to start typing.

As you type, Go2 will dynamically build the menu below the search field based on the results of your search. The first four results of the search will have hotkeys associated with them numbering from 1-4, so to launch the third result, you can click on the result with your mouse, or you can press the hotkey “⌘3”.

As you use the Go2 search menu, the previous four selections are available immediately after clicking the status bar item. If there are certain bookmarks you launch repeatedly, this should make it a little faster to get to them.

You can open the Go2 search menu without your hands leaving the keyboard. To do so, open Go2’s preferences window, select the "Keyboard" tab and enter a hotkey. This hotkey is system-wide, so even if you are working in another application you can press the hotkey to bring up the quick search menu and launch one of your bookmarks.

Go2 also lets you arrow down out of the quick search field, so you can use the arrow keys to select the bookmark you want.
